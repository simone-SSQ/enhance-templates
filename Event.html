South Burnett Community Cabinet

Date: Sunday 7 June, 2026
Time: 2pm
Location: Kingaroy, South Burnett region

Register

What's involved

A Community Cabinet meeting gives you an opportunity to ask questions, raise issues, share ideas and meet with Queensland Government representative.

Meeting agenda

  • 15-minute meeting with a Cabinet minister
  • networking afternoon tea with the Premier, ministers and government representatives.

You can choose to attend either the 15-minute meeting or afternoon tea, or both. You will also be able to nominate the minister you wish to meet. The Premier may not always be able to attend a Community Cabinet meeting. You will still have an opportunity to speak with a minister or government representative.

What to bring

  • Proof of ticket or registration
  • Photo ID
  • Key speaking points and any relevant documents or background information the minister can takeaway.

Who can attend

You can attend the Community Cabinet meeting if you are:

  • Individuals
  • Groups of up to 3 people
  • Deputations

Cost

Free.

Contact

Contact the Department of Premier and Cabinet by emailing communitycabinet@premiers.qld.gov.au.
