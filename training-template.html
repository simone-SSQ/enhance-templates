Mediation level 1 training

Learn how to facilitate a mediation process in our Mediation Level 1 training.

In short

Learn essential communication, tactical and cultural sensitivity skills to understand conflict resolution at a deeper level. Equip yourself with powerful national standard-negotiation frameworks to deliver win-win outcomes for any client.

Enrol now

  • in-person training sessions—38 hours (equivalent to 5 days)

What you will learn

The course covers professional knowledge, skills and ethical principles that accredited mediators are expected to use.

Topics

  • Principles/philosophy of mediation
  • Understanding conflict
  • Facilitative model of mediation
  • Intake process
  • Communication skills
  • Negotiation frameworks
  • Cultural considerations, inclusivity accessibility, and diversity
  • Managing difficult behaviours and responses
  • Trauma-informed practice and bias
  • Importance of continuing professional development
  • Negotiation skills

How you will be assessed

Closed-book written exam person (1 hour)

The assessment is optional and only required if you want become a nationally accredited mediator.

Awards you get

Certificate of training award.

Nationally recognised by the Australian mediator and dispute resolution accreditation standards (AMDRAS).
