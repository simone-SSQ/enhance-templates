Introduction to Conflict Coaching

Part of the Conflict Resolution Training topic

This course helps you develop practical skills to manage and resolve conflicts effectively, prevent future conflicts, and build your dispute resolution practice.

On this page

  • Course details
  • Who can enrol
  • How to enrol
  • What you will learn
  • How you will be assessed
  • Contact

In short

Introduction to Conflict Coaching is a 2-day course designed to help you support individuals and managers in managing or resolving conflict. You'll learn practical strategies to address conflict, improve communication skills, and prevent future issues.

Enrol now

Course details

Important information about Introduction to Conflict Coaching

Delivery

In person over 2 days.

Start dates

Currently, no upcoming dates are scheduled.

Location

In-person training sessions are held at:
South Queensland Dispute Resolution Centre
Level 26, 400 George Street,
Brisbane
Qld 4000

Cost

$980.90

Who can enrol

You can enrol in Introduction to Conflict Coaching if:

  • you want to develop skills to help others manage or resolve conflict
  • you're interested in improving your dispute resolution practice.

How to enrol

Step 1: Complete the application form

Fill out the application form with your details.

Enrol now

Step 2: Submit your application

Step 3: Pay the fee

We'll send you a payment link after receiving your application. The course fee is $980.90.

Step 4: Receive your enrolment confirmation

Once your payment is processed, we will send you a confirmation email with further details about the course.

What you will learn

This course covers the following topics:

  • philosophy of conflict coaching
  • effective process for conflict coaching
  • key skills in conflict coaching.

How you will be assessed

There are no formal assessments for this course.

Contact

Contact the Dispute Resolution Branch on (07) 3738 7015 or email mediation.training@justice.qld.gov.au
